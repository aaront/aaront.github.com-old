What is it?

Pinnned is simple and lightweight social bookmarking client for the HP TouchPad.

Pinnned (not a typo) is meant to be a quick way to access your bookmarks. Whether you were browsing something at work and want to read it later or you want access to your favourite sites on the road, Pinnned will get you your content as soon as possible.

Pinnned features Pinboard and Delicious support, with support for more services coming shortly.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

I can't sign into my Delicious account
Delicious recently changed owners. Your Yahoo! credentials probably won't work, you need to visit the Delicious site and find your Delicious account username and password.
Why don't you have feature X?
Pinnned was conceived and built in a little under a month. The new Enyo framework is powerful, easy, and fun. New features will be added all the time, and if you buy now, you'll get them free of charge.
Is there a free version?
Short answer: no. Long answer: No, but it's definitely an idea in the works. As the paid version gains more features, I may split off development and make a free version.
